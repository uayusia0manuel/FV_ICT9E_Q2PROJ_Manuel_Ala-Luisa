Fried Bangus

Ingredients

  • 1 Large boneless bangus milkfish; scales removed, cleaned and butterflied
  • 1/2 Cup white vinegar
  • 4 Cloves garlic crushed
  • 1/2 Teaspoon whole peppercorn
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt
  • 1 Cup cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Mix vinegar, garlic, whole peppercorns, and salt in a bowl. Mix.
  2. In a large, deep dish, pour the mixture and then place the fish with the sliced side down (skin side up). Cover it and refrigerate. Soak for 8 to 12 hours.
  3. Warm the cooking oil in a skillet. Cook both sides of the bangus over medium heat until it achieves a medium brown color (note: this should take about 5 minutes per side). Caution: the oil tends to splatter due to the marinade's liquid. Ensure that the pan is covered, but allow a small gap for the steam to escape.
  4. Accompany with flavored vinegar (sinamak).
  5. Distribute and savor!
