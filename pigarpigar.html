Pigar pigar

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef, sliced thinly
  • 150g Beef liver, sliced thinly
  • 1 Large onions, sliced
  • 4 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tsp salt
  • 1 Tsp black pepper
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, season beef slices and liver for a minimum of one hour in soy sauce, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Now take a wok and pour in plenty of oil suitable for deep-frying the beef. Warm the oil until it approaches its smoking point, then incorporate the beef and liver slices.
  3. Once the edges of each beef slice are crispy (the edges will burn first), take out the beef with a slotted spoon and place it on a metal sieve (to let oil drain) then promptly add the sliced onions and stir, using the residual heat to cook the onions.
